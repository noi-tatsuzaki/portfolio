body_en: |
  On June 14, 2024, I founded **[ADvance Lab Co., Ltd.](https://www.linoa-lab.co.jp/)**, a company built around a simple but deeply personal idea:

  to create future concepts driven by curiosity.



  This company was established as the operating body of **[ADvance Lab](https://adlab.lne.st/)**, a next-generation research community that I had been involved in conceptualizing since 2023. Through that journey, I became increasingly aware of structural gaps surrounding young researchers — limited access to research environments for middle and high school students, discontinuities in research opportunities during early university education, and a lack of global collaboration among peers of the same generation.



  ADvance Lab was originally created to address these challenges. However, I came to believe that building a sustainable system required more than just a community — it needed an organizational structure that could continuously support, expand, and connect these efforts to society. That realization led to the founding of ADvance Lab Co., Ltd.



  What makes this initiative unique is that it is driven by the very generation it aims to support. By having next-generation researchers directly involved in its operation, we aim to bring flexibility, urgency, and authenticity into how research ecosystems are designed and evolved.



  Our vision is:

  "To create future concepts through curiosity."



  And our mission is:

  "To become a nucleus that generates crystallized knowledge beyond time."



  Through the operation of ADvance Lab and collaborative projects between researchers and companies, we aim to bridge knowledge creation and real-world implementation — not as separate processes, but as a continuous, living system.



  This is not just the launch of a company.

  For me, it is the beginning of building an infrastructure where curiosity itself can shape the future.



  Learn more: <https://www.linoa-lab.co.jp/news-detail/establishment-release>
